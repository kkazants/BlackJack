Welcome to Blackjack Simulator!

Blackjack Simulator automatically plays for user based on information given and experience level chosen.

The Rules:

  • Played with one 52-card decks.
  • 2-10 are worth face value.
  • K,Q and J are worth 10.
  • A is either 1 or 11.
  • To win: having higher total without going over 21.
  • Double Down allowed only if player has only 2 cards.
  • ** No Insurance is considered in this simulator. **

Betting

  • The pay out is 3-2, so a 2-card 21 on $5 bet wins $7.50
  • If dealer and player has 2-card 21, the hand pushes (ties) and player gets original bet back.
  • If dealer goes 21 on 3 or more cards and player has a 2-card 21, player gets blackjack 3-2 payoff.

Dealer Rules:

Dealer must hit until total value of cards are 17 or more. Dealer stands on all 17.

Experience Levels:

  • Beginner Strategy:
    • Beginner player plays by same rules the dealer plays.
  • Intermediate Strategy:
    • Intermediate plays based on this table:
